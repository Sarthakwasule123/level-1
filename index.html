About Shivaji Maharaj

Shivaji Maharaj, born on February 19, 1630, was a Maratha warrior king and the founder of the Maratha Empire in western India. He is renowned for his military acumen and innovative strategies in warfare, as well as his contributions to statecraft. Shivaji's reign left an enduring legacy in the history of India.
